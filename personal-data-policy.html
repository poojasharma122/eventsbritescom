Personal Data Policy

Events Brites ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Personal Data Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you interact with our website located at www.eventsbrites.com (the "Site").

1. What Personal Data We Collect

We may collect personal data directly from you or through automated means, including:

  • Name and email address when you submit a contact form, sign up for our newsletter, or submit an event
  • Event information when submitted for listing (event name, description, dates, venue)
  • Technical data, such as your IP address, browser type, device type, and usage patterns, collected via cookies or analytics tools

We do not collect payment or financial data, as Events Brites does not process transactions.

2. How Your Data Is Used

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to contact inquiries
  • To evaluate and process event submissions
  • To send newsletters and updates (only if you opt in)
  • To monitor and improve website performance
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our Site

We do not use your personal data for automated decision-making or profiling.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Your consent (e.g., when you subscribe to the newsletter)
  • Our legitimate interest in managing and improving the Site
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Your request for information or services

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose it was collected for, or to comply with applicable legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

  • Contact submissions: Retained for up to 12 months
  • Newsletter data: Retained until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics data: Retained as per third-party provider policies (e.g., Google Analytics)

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data against accidental loss, unauthorized access, or unlawful processing. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Sharing of Personal Data

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your data may be shared with:

  • Trusted service providers, such as email platforms or analytics services, strictly for the purpose of operating the Site
  • Legal authorities, if required by law or to protect legal rights

All third-party providers are required to handle your data securely and in compliance with relevant data protection laws.

7. International Transfers

Our website may be hosted on servers located outside your jurisdiction. By using the Site, you understand that your data may be transferred and processed in other countries, including Canada and the United States.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to certain types of processing
  • Request a copy of your data in portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@eventsbrites.com.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and to analyze traffic. You can disable cookies through your browser settings. For more information, please refer to our [Cookie Policy] (if applicable or include inline if needed).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Personal Data Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review it regularly.
